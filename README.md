## How to Play
1. Click "START RACE" button
2. Use brake (left) and accelerate (right) buttons
3. OR tilt your mobile device to steer
4. Avoid collisions with other vehicles
5. Pass cars to increase your score

## Controls
- **Brake Button (Left)**: Slow down the car
- **Accelerate Button (Right)**: Speed up the car
- **Tilt Controls**: Tilt mobile left/right to change lanes
- **Keyboard (Desktop)**: Arrow keys for testing
